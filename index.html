It's Your Choice

Pros

Many people, or the average Joe, say that we have our own choices in this world, and I can agree to that. It's a positive thing about being a citizen of the U.S.A. Choice is very easy, but can turn difficult for people under stress or in difficult situations, but the input is still the same. Choices are about: 0 or 1, Yes or No, Up or Down, Left or Right, Black or White. Choice, like voting for our own representative, is a great way for anyone to become involved with the government. We have the ability to start our own lives, the choice of picking what you want when you want. That is a huge privilege.

Cons

The minority, people who are WOKE understand the fact that when we vote, we don't really have a choice on who we vote for as our representative. The Electoral College chooses for us based on our votes during the elections. Another reason why choice is bad is because not many people can even make a choice for themselves. Many people in this world are simpletons who need to be shown the correct way to go in life. “Some men are born to rule, but most men are born to be ruled” - David Rockefeller. Not everyone is born to choose their own life, but others are born with chances.

How It Impacts Govt.

The principle of Allowing Choices is very crucial in our Government because it allows what the Founding Fathers wanted for us: Freedom of Choice.
